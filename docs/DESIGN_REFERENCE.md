# Design Reference – Middletown Medical Migration

This project is a **migration of [middletownmedical.com](https://middletownmedical.com/)** to a new design and UI.

**Target design system:** **[Duly Health and Care](https://dulyhealthandcare.com)** (dulyhealthandcare).

All “Duly-style” references in the codebase mean: match the look, layout, and patterns of Duly Health and Care’s website, applied to Middletown Medical’s content and brand where appropriate.

---

## What this means in practice

- **Content source:** Middletown Medical (existing site).
- **UI/UX reference:** Duly Health and Care (navigation, typography, colors, components, page structure).
- **Brand:** Middletown Medical name, logo, and content; visual treatment (colors, spacing, components) aligned with Duly.

When implementing or reviewing pages (e.g. Services, Resources, Locations, Providers), use Duly Health and Care as the design reference for consistency across the migrated site.
